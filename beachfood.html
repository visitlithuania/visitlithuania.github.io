Food

Smoked Fish

Nida, being a seaside town, is all about local seafood. One of the most popular local dishes is smoked fish, usually sold to 'take away'and not one to be missed. You can buy it from locals, preparing the fish at their homes in the open

Nidus

A relaxed yet elegant and stylish place to eat at any time of the day, featuring an outside grill to prepare fresh local seafood, giving you a chance to watch the chef work their magic

Nerija

If you are up for a fine dining experience, Nerija is the place to be. Excelent service, immaculate presentation and an extraordinary combination of flavours is guaranteed every time
